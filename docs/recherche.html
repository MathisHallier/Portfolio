Research in AI

Analyzing complex datasets presents a significant challenge. A thorough analysis can greatly enhance an institution's outcomes. The process always begins with data visualization, which is crucial for understanding the dataset. However, visualizing complex datasets can be difficult and may lead to incorrect hypotheses.

The new method I worked on during my Research experience in Warsaw allows the visualization of any dataset. We were three taking part of this project, two supervisors and I. They guided me and I was in charge of creating the theory, of the implementation with python but also of the presentation of the tool. This method based on clustering (AI) algorithms allows the user visualize complex datasets in different domains such as biological, finance, real estate and more.

The following examples are showing the rendered visualization for real or toy datasets.

Visualization of a dataset about diabete :

This is a visualization of a dataset with 6 variables related to diabetes. The graph was generated using the KMeans algorithm. Each ball represents a population and is colored based on the average insulin level. Two branches can be observed: the left branch with a low insulin level and the right branch with a high insulin level. This type of visualization can quickly reveal two types of diabetes.
